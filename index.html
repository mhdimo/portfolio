  • Badge Weighting System V2

    BWS-V2 or Badge Weighting System V2 is a system that i've designed in the past weeks for the game osu!.
    The formula consist of putting weight (expressed in float) to badges awarded in the time span of 1 year subdiving them in the following way:

    • from 0 to 3 months, the weight applied is 0.2;
    • from 3 to 6 months, the weight applied is 0.6;
    • from 6 to 9 months, the weight applied is 0.9;
    • for badges older than 9 months, the weight applies is 0.01

    This project is still WIP (Work In Progress).
